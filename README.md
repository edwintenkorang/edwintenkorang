# About-Me

# Hi, I'm Edwin Tenkorang

I am an Electrical Engineering PhD student at Texas A&M University working at the intersection of **power systems, software engineering, cyber-physical systems, and real-time simulation**.

My research focuses on building automated pipelines and digital testbeds for studying modern power systems with high penetration of distributed energy resources, including **EV charging, PV, battery energy storage systems, demand response, and cyber-physical attack scenarios**. I am especially interested in roles where power systems knowledge is combined with strong software, simulation, data, and infrastructure skills.


## Technical Focus Areas

### Power Systems Modeling and Simulation

I work with distribution and transmission system modeling tools to analyze power system behavior under changing grid conditions, DER integration, and cyber-physical disturbances.
